# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for Students

- Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Always verify links before clicking on them
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Avoid downloading apps from untrusted websites
- Regularly update your phone and software for security patches
- Do not connect to public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Check sender identity before responding to suspicious emails or messages
- Immediately report fraud through the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in)
- Call the cybercrime helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
