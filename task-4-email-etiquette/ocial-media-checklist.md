# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s

- Maintain a professional profile on platforms like LinkedIn
- Share academic achievements and certifications
- Verify information before sharing posts
- Respect others while communicating online
- Adjust privacy settings to protect personal data

## Don’ts

- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not share personal or financial information publicly
- Avoid spreading misinformation or rumors
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
- Avoid using unprofessional language in public posts
